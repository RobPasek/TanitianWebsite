Entertainment & Activities on Taniti

Visitors come to Taniti for beaches, rainforest, and the volcano, but there is also a wide range of cultural, recreational, and nightlife activities available across the island.

Outdoor & Nature Activities

Many visitors enjoy outdoor adventures, including:

  • Relaxing on sandy and rocky beaches.
  • Exploring the lush tropical rainforest.
  • Visiting Taniti’s active volcano.
  • Going on boat or bus tours of the island.
  • Hiking in the mountainous interior.
  • Snorkeling in clear coastal waters.
  • Zip-lining in the rainforest.
  • Going on chartered fishing tours.

Culture & Nightlife

Taniti also offers a variety of cultural and entertainment options:

  • Visiting a local history museum.
  • Exploring art galleries.
  • Playing at an arcade or going bowling.
  • Seeing a movie at the local theater.
  • Visiting several pubs, including a microbrewery.
  • Dancing at a new dance club.
  • Taking helicopter rides for aerial views of the island.

A nine-hole golf course is expected to open next year, adding another leisure activity for visitors.

Merriton Landing

Many of Taniti’s entertainment venues and activities are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. This area is especially convenient for tourists who enjoy walking between attractions.
