Food & Dining

Where to Eat on Taniti

Taniti offers a small but diverse restaurant scene, from local fish and rice dishes to familiar American-style meals and Pan-Asian cuisine.

Restaurants on Taniti

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants:

  • Five restaurants serve mostly local fish and rice.
  • Three restaurants serve American-style meals.
  • Two restaurants serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Many pubs and a microbrewery also serve food, providing additional casual dining options, especially in and around Merriton Landing.

Local Flavors

Visitors often come to Taniti to enjoy freshly caught seafood and simple, satisfying meals made with local ingredients. Trying fish and rice prepared in traditional ways is a highlight for many guests.

Plan Around Holidays

Taniti observes a large number of national holidays, and many restaurants close on these days. Visitors should check hours in advance and plan accordingly, especially if traveling during local holiday periods.

Quick Tips

Popular dining areas are located in Taniti City and around Merriton Landing. Reservations may be helpful during busy tourist seasons and on weekends.
