Discover Taniti

A Small Tropical Island with Big Adventures

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific with sandy and rocky beaches, a safe harbor, lush rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. The island has an indigenous population of about 20,000 and is transitioning from a fishing-and-agriculture economy to a growing tourist destination.

Why Visit Taniti?

Most tourists spend their time in Taniti City and around Yellow Leaf Bay, enjoying native architecture, white sandy beaches, and access to the rainforest and volcano.

  • Relax on beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.
  • Explore lush tropical rainforest and mountain trails.
  • Visit an active volcano on guided excursions.
  • Enjoy a variety of restaurants, lodging, and entertainment options.
