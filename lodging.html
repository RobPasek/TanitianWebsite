Where to Stay

Lodging Options on Taniti

Taniti offers a wide variety of lodging, from an inexpensive hostel to a large four-star resort, along with many family-owned hotels and bed and breakfasts.

Types of Lodging

  • An inexpensive hostel for budget-conscious travelers.
  • Many small, family-owned hotels with local character.
  • A growing number of bed and breakfasts.
  • One large, four-star resort with more full-service amenities.

Quality & Regulation

All types of lodging on Taniti are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government. This helps ensure that accommodations meet safety and quality standards for visitors.

Location Considerations

Many tourists choose to stay in or near Taniti City or the rapidly developing Merriton Landing area, which offer easy access to beaches, restaurants, and entertainment.
